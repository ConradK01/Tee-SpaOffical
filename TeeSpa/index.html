Swedish Massage

Using lotion or oil, our massage therapists typically begin with broad general strokes and then transition to specific strokes to address problem areas.

Learn More

Shiatsu Massage

A form of Japanese bodywork, shiatsu massage involves localized pressure using the therapist's fingers (or sometimes hands and elbows), applied in a rhythmic sequence along the body. Each point is held for about two to eight seconds.

Learn More

Deep Tissue Massage

Deep tissue massage is a focused, therapeutic massage that targets muscle knots (also known as "adhesions") and specific problem areas in the deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue.
